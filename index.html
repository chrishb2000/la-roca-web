Our Story

Welcome to La Roca

Authentic Ecuadorian Cuisine Since Day One

At La Roca Restaurant, we bring the authentic flavors of Ecuador to Elizabeth, New Jersey. Our dishes are prepared with traditional recipes passed down through generations, using the freshest ingredients and the most authentic spices.

When you come to La Roca, you're not just a customer, you're family. Every plate served is a way of embracing our culture and sharing it with you.
